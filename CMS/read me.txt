---TEXT---
In the text file the first line corresponds to the title h1.
In the second and the third correspond to the subtile h4 and subtile h5,
The following lines correspond to the paragraph.
---IMG---
In the image file the odd lines are the image path,
 while the even lines are the caption of the image.
---LIST---
With 0 you will have a numbered list while with a 1 an unumbered list
---TABLE---
In the table file the & divides the cells while the rows divide the rows
